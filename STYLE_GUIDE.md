# World Cup Festival Style Guide

## Theme
Use a warm, monument-inspired football festival style. The visual mood should feel sunlit, carved, and ceremonial: pale stone surfaces, sandstone highlights, terracotta energy, dark wood contrast, and deep teal anchors.

## Color Tokens
| Token | Hex | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `--stone-mist` | `#d3d8cc` | Soft panels, subtle highlights, quiet supporting accents |
| `--sandstone` | `#c9a276` | Primary buttons, badges, active states, countdown emphasis |
| `--terracotta` | `#a5664c` | Featured accents, alerts, section markers, warm emphasis |
| `--carved-umber` | `#704638` | Secondary text, borders, warm gradients |
| `--deep-teal` | `#244b4d` | Navigation accents, success states, strong contrast blocks |
| `--espresso` | `#351a12` | Primary text, dark headers, footer/nav surfaces |
| `--ivory` | `#fffaf0` | Card surfaces and text on dark backgrounds |
| `--warm-paper` | `#f6f2e8` | Page background |

## Usage Rules
- Use `--warm-paper` for the overall page background and `--ivory` for cards, panels, forms, and tables.
- Use `--espresso` for primary text and dark structural areas such as the sticky header and group-table headers.
- Use `--sandstone` for primary calls to action, active nav states, tags, and countdown numbers.
- Use `--deep-teal` for status success, important structural accents, and the darkest cool contrast.
- Use `--terracotta` sparingly for feature markers, watch-this accents, and warm emphasis.
- Avoid bright saturated blues, neon greens, and pure yellow; they break the screenshot-inspired theme.

## Typography
- Use Georgia for major headings to keep the page editorial and ceremonial.
- Use Trebuchet MS or Segoe UI for body and interface text.
- Keep letter spacing at `0` for large headings and reserve uppercase tracking for small labels only.

## Components
- Cards use `--ivory` backgrounds, `1px` warm borders, and `8px` radius.
- Buttons use rounded pill shapes, bold text, and clear contrast.
- Badges should use palette-based gradients instead of individual team colors.
- Dark bands should pair `--espresso` or `--deep-teal` with `--ivory` text.
- Gradients should combine screenshot colors only: teal, umber, terracotta, and sandstone.

## Imagery
- Use real football photography, but tint it with warm umber, sandstone, and deep teal overlays.
- Avoid cold blue stadium treatments unless heavily balanced with the palette.
- Hero images should have enough dark space for readable ivory text.

## Accessibility
- Keep body text on light surfaces in `--espresso` or `--carved-umber`.
- Keep text on dark surfaces in `--ivory`.
- Use `--sandstone` focus outlines so keyboard navigation remains visible.
